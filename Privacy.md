# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 16, 2026**

**SolSplitter** (also known as **SolSplit**) is a simple split-screen launcher for Android devices, particularly optimized for the Solana Seeker.

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect **no personal information**.

- The app does not require any accounts, login, or user registration.
- All data (saved app pairs) is stored **locally on your device** only.
- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics.

### 2. Permissions
SolSplitter requires only the following standard Android permissions:
- **Query installed apps** — To display the list of your installed apps.
- **Launch other apps** — To open apps in split-screen mode.

No other permissions are requested or used.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do **not** share any data with third parties.  
The app contains **no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs**.

### 4. Local Storage
Saved app pairs are stored locally using Android’s SharedPreferences. You can clear this data at any time through your device settings.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this repository.
